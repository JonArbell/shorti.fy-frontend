How to Use Shorti.fy?

  1. Sign In or Sign Up - Create an account or log in to access the URL shortener. You can also use Google OAuth2 for a quick sign-in.
  2. Enter Your Long URL - Paste the link you want to shorten.
  3. Customize Your Link (Optional)
    • Set a custom short URL.
    • Add an expiration date to automatically deactivate the link.
    • Enable password protection for private access.
  4. Generate & Copy Your Short Link - Click "Shorten" to create your unique URL. Copy and share it anywhere!
  5. Track & Manage Your Links
    • View click analytics (e.g., total clicks, location, and device details).
    • Edit or delete existing short links from your dashboard.
    • Download a QR code for easier sharing (coming soon).
