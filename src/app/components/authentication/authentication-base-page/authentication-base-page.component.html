How to Use Shorti.fy?

  1. Sign In or Sign Up - Create an account or use Google OAuth2.
  2. Shorten a URL - Paste your link, customize it (optional), and click "Shorten."
  3. ShareCopy your short link and share it.
  4. Generate & Copy Your Short Link - Click "Shorten" to create your unique URL. Copy and share it anywhere!
  5. Manage - Track clicks, edit, delete, or download a QR code (coming soon).
